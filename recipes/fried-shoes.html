Fried Shoes

Description

You've seen deep fried butter, deep fried ice cream, and deep friend twinkies.

Logically, the next step is deep fried shoe.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Shoe
  • Buttermilk
  • AP Flour

Steps

  1. Heat oil to 350 F.
  2. Make batter with buttermilk and AP flour.
  3. Dip shoe in batter.
  4. Fry shoe in oil for 15 min.
