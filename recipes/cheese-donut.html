Cheese Donuts

Description

Everybody loves cheese and donuts. Now introducing Cheese Donuts!

Countless people have been disappointed that donuts don't have enough cheese.

Follow me in this cheesey journey.

Ingredients

  • 83 slices, Kraft American Singles
  • 83 glazed donuts

Steps

  1. Gingerly place a slice of cheese on a donut.
  2. Microwave for 18 minutes.
