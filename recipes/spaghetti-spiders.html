Spaghetti Spiders

Description

Spice up your Halloween this year with the internet's most beloved dish.

Buttery noodles paired with snappy Ballpark hotdogs will surely put a smile on your kids' faces.

Ingredients

  • 4 hotdogs
  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • Ketchup
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cut up hotdogs.
  2. Insert 4 spaghetti noodles in each hotdog segment.
  3. Boil hotdogs and spaghetti for 10 min.
  4. Drain water and put 2 tablespoons of butter in pot.
  5. Serve with ketchup.
